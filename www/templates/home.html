Our Goals

We chose to make an app that makes it easy to play music in public settings.


What we mean by this is, we eliminate the need to have someone assume the cumbersome role as "music man".


Our solution is to simply let the people hear what they want! Just hook your phone up to the speakers, and your party-goers will add tunes to the playlist themselves!


Now, no one has to go through the trouble of finding the DJ to request a song

How It's Done

It's a relatively simple idea: the host (or whoever hooks their phone up to the speakers) will literally "host" a playlist.


Anyone who wants to contribute to the playlist will need to connect to the host. And that's it!
